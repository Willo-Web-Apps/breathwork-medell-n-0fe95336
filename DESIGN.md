# Design DNA — Aliento

## Visual Personality: warm-luxurious
## Layout: storytelling
## Typography: elegant-serif

### Color Palette
- Primary (Terracotta): #8B3A2A → oklch(0.43 0.13 30)
- Accent (Warm Gold): #C8872A → oklch(0.63 0.13 65)
- Surface (Warm Off-White): #F5EDE3 → oklch(0.95 0.02 65)
- Text (Deep Warm Brown): oklch(0.22 0.04 40)

### Font Pairing
- Display: Cormorant Garamond — weights 300, 400, 600, 700 (luxury editorial, old-world warmth)
- Body: Montserrat — weights 300, 400, 500 (clean, modern complement)

### Unique Design Choices
1. **Immersive atmospheric hero**: Full-bleed layered gradient (terracotta glow from bottom-left, gold radiance from top-right) with a film-grain noise overlay — evokes Medellín's golden afternoon light.
2. **Editorial breathing-room layouts**: Alternating generous/tight section padding creates rhythm — sections expand and contract like breath itself. Wide pull-quotes in Cormorant italic float in warm whitespace.
3. **Two distinct CTA personalities**: "Book a Private Session" = deep terracotta (intimate, personal); "Join the Next Reset" = warm gold (luminous, communal) — two emotional registers, visually distinct throughout.
4. **Nordika rooftop section**: Layered warm gradient background with frosted-glass card — evoking the sensory richness of sauna, cold plunge, and open Medellín city views.
5. **Organic, non-grid layout rhythm**: Story-telling flow rather than columns — full-width text moments, asymmetric text+space splits, large numerals as visual anchors.
